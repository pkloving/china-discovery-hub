HEYTEA (喜茶) is the brand that turned Chinese new-tea culture into a global
phenomenon — meticulously sourced fresh fruit, cheese-foam toppings, and
shop interiors that wouldn't look out of place in Tokyo. Expansion into
the UK, US and Southeast Asia from 2023 onward.
