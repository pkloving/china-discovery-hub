Pleco is widely regarded as the most powerful Chinese dictionary for non-native
learners. Point your phone at a sign or menu and OCR will look up every
character instantly. The free tier alone outclasses most paid dictionaries;
paid add-ons include classical Chinese and full Oxford/Tuttle bilingual sets.
