Xiachufang is what a regular Chinese person opens when they want to cook
红烧肉 or 番茄炒蛋. Recipes are crowdsourced, photos are unfiltered home-kitchen
reality, and the algorithm pushes whatever's seasonal and trending. The UI
is Chinese only — browser translate gets you 70% of the way — but the
value isn't "use it tonight," it's seeing what Chinese home cooking looks
like outside the YouTube version.
