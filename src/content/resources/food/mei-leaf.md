Mei Leaf is half tea shop, half tea school. Don Mei's video library runs
through sheng vs shou pu'er, gongfu brewing, what actually makes a Da Hong
Pao a Da Hong Pao — hundreds of free hour-long videos that move you from
"tea is hot leaf water" to "I know what I'm drinking." The shop ships
globally and sources directly from Chinese tea regions.
