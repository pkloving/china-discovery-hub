Founded by Taylor Holliday after years of sourcing trips to Sichuan, The
Mala Market specializes in single-origin, traceable Chinese ingredients
that mainstream Asian groceries don't carry — pre-aged Pixian doubanjiang,
Hanyuan peppercorns from the right slopes, year-vintage Shaoxing wine. Each
product comes with a long-form blog post on provenance and production. If
you're cooking Fuchsia Dunlop's recipes seriously, this is where the
ingredients come from.
