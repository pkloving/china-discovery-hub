88rising started as a YouTube channel for "the rest of the world to hear
Asia and Asian America" and is now one of the most influential platforms
for diaspora artists in pop. The site, festivals (Head in the Clouds) and
roster offer a real cultural read on Asian-diaspora identity in the 2020s.
