VibeHanzi flips the usual textbook approach on its head: instead of memorizing
abstract vocabulary, you learn hanzi through the actual signs, plaques and
landmarks you'd encounter in Shanghai and Beijing. Each spot comes with a short
piece of lore, then a quick match game that drills the characters in context.
The result is a small, focused hanzi set that's actually useful on a trip — no
pinyin-first drills, no "the cat is on the table" sentences.
