Taobao is the consumer face of China's e-commerce machine — anything you can
think of, listed by tens of thousands of sellers, often shippable next-day
within China. Foreign-facing options exist (mostly through Taobao agents and
the Tmall Global subdomain); for the curious, the platform's livestream
shopping format is required cultural literacy.
