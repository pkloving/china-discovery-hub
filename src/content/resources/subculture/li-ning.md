Li-Ning's brand revival from 2018 onward is the canonical guochao success
story — Chinese typography, traditional motifs, and a deliberate move
upmarket via streetwear collaborations. Their Paris/New York Fashion Week
shows reset Western expectations of "made in China" fashion.
