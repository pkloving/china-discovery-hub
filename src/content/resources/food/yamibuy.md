Yamibuy is where Chinese cooking blogs send their North American readers for
hard-to-find ingredients — proper Pixian doubanjiang, Sichuan peppercorns,
mochi, instant hot pot kits. Ships across the US with cold-chain for some
items; UK and other markets get a more limited but growing selection.
