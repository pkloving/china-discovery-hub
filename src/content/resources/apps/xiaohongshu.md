Xiaohongshu (literally "Little Red Book", branded internationally as RedNote)
sits somewhere between Instagram, Pinterest and Yelp. Young Chinese use it
to research everything from restaurants to skincare. The 2025 TikTok refugee
moment turned it into an unexpected cultural exchange platform.
