Dunlop trained at the Sichuan Higher Institute of Cuisine in the 1990s and
has been writing seriously about Chinese food longer than any other
English-language voice. Her site collects book information, occasional blog
posts, and notes from her ongoing China trips. Start with her books
(*The Food of Sichuan*, *Land of Fish and Rice*, *Invitation to a Banquet*)
and use the site to keep up with the writing she does between them.
