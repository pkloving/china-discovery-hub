Wei moved from Beijing to the UK in 2007 and started Red House Spice to fix
a gap she kept hitting: English Chinese recipes that didn't account for what
non-American cooks could actually buy. Her dumpling, scallion pancake and
braising posts are textbook — measured, step-by-step photographed, tested.
Especially useful if you're outside North America and Yamibuy isn't an
option.
