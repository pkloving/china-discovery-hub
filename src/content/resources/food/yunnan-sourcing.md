Yunnan Sourcing is Mei Leaf's natural companion: where Mei Leaf gives you
breadth and tea education, YS goes deep on one thing — pu'er — with the
inventory and provenance detail hardcore drinkers want. Scott Wilson has
been sourcing from small Yunnan farms for two decades; the catalog runs
from $5 daily drinkers to $500 aged collectibles, all with notes that
explain why this one differs from that one.
