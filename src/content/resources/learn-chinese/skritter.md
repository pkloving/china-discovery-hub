Skritter is the rare app built for the part of Chinese most learners avoid:
actually writing characters by hand. You draw on screen and the app grades
stroke order, direction, and accuracy — then schedules each character with
spaced repetition tuned to your error rate. Decks ship for every major
textbook (Integrated Chinese, HSK 1-6, New Practical Chinese Reader) so it
slots into whatever curriculum you're already using. Not cheap, but for
anyone serious about reading and writing — not just reading-by-tapping —
it's the closest thing to a teacher watching over your shoulder.
