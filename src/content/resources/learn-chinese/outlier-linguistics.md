Outlier's Chinese Character Masterclass and the companion Pleco add-on are
in a class of their own. Built by linguists with a paleography background,
each entry traces a character back through bronze and oracle-bone forms,
distinguishes meaningful components from phonetic ones, and corrects the
folk-etymology myths that pollute most "character story" resources. If
you've ever wondered why 妈 has 马 in it or how 法 stopped meaning "water
leaving" — this is where the real answer lives. Best paired with Pleco.
