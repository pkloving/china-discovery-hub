eChinacities is one of the older expat-in-China hubs. Today its strongest use
is the jobs board (ESL, marketing, editor roles across multiple cities) and
the steadily-updated articles on practical China-life topics — work permit
renewals, banking changes, where to find apartments without a Chinese ID.
