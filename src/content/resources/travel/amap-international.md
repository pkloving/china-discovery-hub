Google Maps is famously unreliable inside China (offset projections, missing
transit). Amap (Gaode 高德) is what locals use. The English mode has limits
— some POI names stay in Chinese — but bus and subway routing is excellent
and ride-hailing integrates with DiDi out of the box.
