Alipay launched as Alibaba's escrow service for Taobao buyers and is now half
of mainland China's payments rails. For tourists, the "Tour Pass" feature
lets you load a foreign credit card and pay anywhere QR codes are accepted —
the single biggest practical unlock for visiting China today.
