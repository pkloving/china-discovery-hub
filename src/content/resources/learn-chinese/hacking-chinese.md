Hacking Chinese isn't a course or an app — it's the meta-layer above them.
Olle Linge, a Swedish Chinese teacher and PhD researcher, has spent over a
decade writing about how learners *should* spend their time: how to actually
practice tones, why most flashcard strategies fail, how to read above your
level without burning out, what to do when you plateau at intermediate.
Every article is grounded in research and his own teaching. Read three
posts here and you'll save yourself months of wasted effort.
