Donghua — Chinese-made animation — has gone from "anime imitator" to its own
distinctive aesthetic and storytelling tradition. Bilibili is where the
biggest titles premiere and where the fan community lives. The English-
subbed releases are usually a season behind, but most flagship series have
official subtitles on YouTube too.
