SCMP, founded in 1903, is the most internationally-read English-language
newspaper covering China and Hong Kong. The breadth — politics, tech,
business, lifestyle — and the Hong Kong vantage make it a consistent
counterweight to mainland and Western outlets. Some content paywalled.
