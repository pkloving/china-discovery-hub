Mandy Lee started Lady and Pups while stuck in Beijing in the early 2010s,
hating the city and channeling the frustration into the most
technique-obsessed Asian food blog on the English internet. Her char siu,
mapo, ramen and dumpling recipes are tested obsessively — and she'll tell
you which step you're tempted to skip and exactly why doing so will ruin
it. The book *The Art of Escapism Cooking* collects the best of it.
